About

Hi, I'm Sage.

Thanks for stopping by. This little corner of the internet is where I write about the things that quietly take up space in most of our heads — love, mindset, the strange little curiosities that catch on and spread before anyone quite knows why.

I'm not a therapist, a relationship coach, or an expert of any kind — just someone who reads, notices, and writes things down. Some posts are personal stories, like the one you may have just read. Others are me digging into a trend everyone's suddenly talking about, trying to figure out what's actually going on underneath it.

What you'll find here

Mostly: honest, first-person writing. I try not to oversell things or promise outcomes I can't back up. If I write about something — a trend, a tool, an experience — it's because it actually made me curious enough to try it or think about it, not because someone paid me to say nice things.

That said, some posts do include affiliate links — meaning if you click through and make a purchase, I may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. I'll always try to be upfront about that. You can read the full details in my Terms & Conditions.

Why I started this

Honestly? I kept having these little moments — reading something, trying something, noticing something — and wanting to talk them through with someone. Writing them down here felt like the next best thing. If even one post makes you feel a little less alone in whatever you're working through, or just gives you something to think about on your commute, that's the whole point.

Get in touch

Questions, feedback, or just want to say hi? Email me at hello@sagespencer.com — I read everything, even if I can't always reply quickly.
