Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Simple promise: We only collect what we need to send your emails. We don’t sell your data.

1) Who we are

This website is operated by Sage Spencer (“we”, “us”, “our”). If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at hello@sagespencer.com.

2) What data we collect

When you join the email list, we may collect:

  • Name (if you provide it)
  • Email address
  • Basic signup details (e.g., time of signup, which form you used)

3) Why we collect it

We use your data to:

  • Send the 21‑Day Email Journey and related emails you requested
  • Manage subscriptions (unsubscribes, preferences)
  • Improve deliverability and prevent abuse/spam

4) Legal basis (GDPR)

We process your information based on consent (you asked to receive emails). You can withdraw your consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.

5) Email service provider (Brevo)

We use Brevo (Sendinblue SAS) to store subscriber details and send emails. Brevo processes your data on our behalf to provide email delivery and list management.

6) How long we keep your data

We keep your subscriber data for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, we will stop sending emails and may retain a minimal record to respect your unsubscribe request.

7) Sharing and selling

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers we use to run the website and send emails (such as Brevo).

8) Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Object to processing in certain situations

To exercise these rights, email hello@sagespencer.com.

9) Cookies and analytics

This site may use minimal cookies required for basic functionality. If we add analytics or advertising in the future, we’ll update this policy and (where required) request consent.

10) Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest version.
